Furious - Unleash Chaos with the Wildest Monkey Adventure

Step into the wild world of Furious, a thrilling action game where every decision shapes your adventure. Designed for players 16 and up, Furious delivers an unforgettable experience that blends exhilarating stunts, deep storytelling, and expansive jungle exploration. Are you ready to unleash chaos?

Your Story, Your path

Every choice you make in Furious has lasting consequences. Will you dominate the jungle with brute force or outsmart your foes with cunning tactics? With numerous branching storylines, no two playthroughs are the same. Form alliances, confront betrayal, and uncover secrets that may change the world around you forever.

Richly Detailed World

Explore an expansive, handcrafted jungle teeming with vibrant flora, hidden temples, and treacherous landscapes. From ancient relics to wild adversaries, every corner of the map holds treasures and trials for those daring enough to seek them.

Dynamic Combat System

Hone your skills in action as you take on powerful enemies and legendary creatures. With customizable abilities, powerful weapons, and unique skills, Furious allows you to develop a playstyle that's uniquely yours. Choose between close-range melee, acrobatic stunts, or stealthy precision as you face each challenge.

Deep Customization

Create a character that reflects your vision. Choose from multiple classes, each with its own unique abilities and skills, and unlock powerful upgrades as you level up. Customize your appearance, gear, and abilities to fit your own legendary playstyle.

Immersive Sound and Visuals

Experience the world of Furious like never before with stunning graphics and immersive soundscapes that transport you into a realm of adventure and excitement. Every battle, every encounter, and every moment of exploration is brought to life with breathtaking detail.
